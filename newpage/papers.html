Saeed Zakeri's Research
My main area of reseach is dynamical systems, especially holomorphic dynamics. I also have strong interests in complex analysis, Riemann surfaces, (trans)quasiconformal mappings, hyperbolic geometry and Kleinian groups, and geometric theory of foliations and laminations.
