Suppose $G$ is a finite abelian group and $n$ divides $|G|$. Let $H=\{ x\in G: x^n=e \}$. Prove that $n$ divides $|H|$ as well.

$G$ is isomorphic to a sum $\ZZ_{n_1} \oplus \ZZ_{n_2} \oplus \cdots \oplus \ZZ_{n_k}$, where each $n_j$ divides $n_{j+1}$.

By the fundamental theorem of finitely generated abelian groups, $$ G \cong \ZZ_{n_1} \oplus \ZZ_{n_2} \oplus \cdots \oplus \ZZ_{n_k}, $$ with $n_1 | n_2 | \cdots | n_k$ and $|G|=n_1n_2\cdots n_k$. An element $x \in G$ satisfies $x^n=e$ if and only if each of its projections $x_j$ in $\ZZ_{n_j}$ satisfies $x_j^n=0$, i.e., if and only if $x_j^{d_j}=0$ where $d_j=\gcd(n_j,n)$. The number of such elements in $\ZZ_{n_j}$ is $\sum_{i|d_j} \phi(i)=d_j$. Thus, $|H|=d_1 \cdots d_k$. Since $n|n_1\cdots n_k$, it follows that $n|d_1\cdots d_k$.
