MATH 704 Syllabus

Here is a preliminary list of the topics that I plan to discuss in the course, subject to minor revision:
  • Infinite products
  • Weierstrass product and factorization theorems
  • Jensen's formula and its applications
  • Blaschke products and their zeros
  • Mittag-Leffler's theorem
  • Elliptic functions
  • Rational and polynomial approximation
  • Natural boundary, overconvergence and Hadamard's gap theorem
  • Analytic continuation, the monodromy theorem
  • Distortion theorems for conformal maps
  • Length-area inequalities, conformal invariants
  • Boundary behavior of conformal maps
  • Non-tangential limits, theorems of Fatou and Riesz brothers
  • Theorems of Bloch, Picard and Montel
  • Holomorphic covering and branched covering maps
  • The uniformization theorem
