MATH 704: Functions of a Complex Variable II

Spring 2025

Description:

This is the continuation of the Fall 2024 course Math 703. We will discuss more advanced topics in complex analysis with some emphasis on the geometric viewpoint. They include zeros of holomorphic functions, interpolation and approximation theorems, the holomorphic extension problem, ranges of holomorphic functions, holomorphic branched covering maps, and the uniformization theorem.
