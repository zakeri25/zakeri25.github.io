Here is a basic Riemann sums program for TI-83.

    PROGRAM: RIEMANN
    :Input ("WHAT IS N? ",N)
    :Input ("LEFT ENDPOINT? ",A)
    :Input ("RIGHT ENDPOINT? ",B)
    :(B-A)/N->D
    :0->R
    :0->L
    :A->X
    :For (I,1,N)
    :L+Y1->L
    :A+I*D->X
    :R+Y1->R
    :End
    :L*D->L
    :R*D->R
    :Disp "LEFT SUM:",L
    :Disp "RIGHT SUM:",R
    :Stop
